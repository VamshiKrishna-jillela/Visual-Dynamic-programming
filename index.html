Assume You are Mario.When You pass by the road You have two choices either you can take upper path or lower path. To Take the upper path you need to jump. Since you are weak, You Can't take two consecutive upper paths. i.e.., When you took upper path, you are forced to take the lower path next time. Each Path contains two certain number of coins. You Can collect coins avaliable in the path you are travelling. Can you collect Maximum number of Coins. More Formally, You are Given a 2D matrix called coins[n][2], where coins[i][0] represents number of coins in lower path of junction i, where coins[i][1] represents number of coins in upper path of junction i. When You took the upper path, next you are forced to take the lower path. you need to maximize the coins.
